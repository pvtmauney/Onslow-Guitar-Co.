Boutique · Handbuilt · Local

Guitars built by local hands, sold by people who know the builder.

Onslow Guitar Co. is a small storefront in Jacksonville, North Carolina with one rule: we only carry instruments made by luthiers working in Onslow County and the surrounding area. No warehouse stock. No two alike.

Our Builders

The luthiers on our wall

Every builder we carry works within driving distance of the shop, and we've stood in every workshop we buy from. The roster is small on purpose — and growing.

Gear

Everything else you leave with

Amps to plug into, strings and cables to keep you playing, straps and accessories for the road. Everything below is in the store; call to hold an item.

Merch

Wear the shop. Sticker the case.

Hats, tees, shop shirts, mugs, koozies, stickers, magnets, and decals — take a little of Onslow Guitar Co. home even if the guitar has to wait.

For Luthiers

Building in eastern North Carolina? We want your instruments on our wall.

  • Consignment or outright purchase — we're flexible while we grow together.
  • Your instruments displayed, humidified, and demoed properly, with your name on the card.
  • A profile on this site with your photos, videos, and story.
  • We handle the retail side so you can stay at the bench.

Introduce yourself

Send a few photos of your recent work and a little about how you build. New and up-and-coming builders are welcome — that's the point.
